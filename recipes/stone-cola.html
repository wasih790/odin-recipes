Stone Cola

Description

When you are in a world with no resources and no cola, you must make Stone Cola.
Stone Cola is a cola made by the character Senku Ishigami, also known as Senku Cola.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bottle of Carbonated Water
  • 4 - 5 Tablespoons of Honey
  • Coriander
  • 1 Lemon/Lime

Steps

  1. Zest the Lime

  2. Crush the coriander and mix it with the lime
    • Shred the coriander leaves and crush them.
    • Now squeeze the lemon and add the juice into the crushed coriander along with the zested lime. Again continue with the crushing until the ingredients form a fine paste.
    • Now strain the mixture that you have created and keep it separate.

  3. Caramelize the honey
    • Take the honey in a saucepan (well a small cup should suffice too) and heat it till it starts boiling.
    • Now simmer the flames and cook it till the honey caramelizes (till it becomes nutty brown, you should get a slightly burnt smell by the time the honey caramelizes).
    • Ideally, youâ€™d leave the honey one for a minute or two at the most. The slightly burnt smell is ideally your cue to stop.

  4. Making the syrup
    • Now mix the strained coriander-lime mixture and the caramelized honey to make the cola syrup.

  5. Summon the cola
    • Final step, take a small quantity of the cola syrup that you have just prepared and mix it with carbonated water to ready your homemade cola.
    • Ideally add around 200 ml of carbonated water to 3-4 tbsp of the cola syrup
